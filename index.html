Our Heritage

Hospitality fit for royalty, rooted in Simrahi

Rising along NH 27 in Simrahi Bazar, Shahi Darbar was built as more than a restaurant — it is a landmark of gathering. Beneath its glowing marble halls and gold-lit ceilings, Hotel Shahi Palace brings together fine multi-cuisine dining, a full-service marriage hall, and warm hospitality under one roof.

Every table is set with intention: contemporary interiors that catch the light, a kitchen that honours North Indian tradition alongside Chinese and Continental favourites, and a team that treats every guest — from a family dinner to a wedding of five hundred — like royalty.
